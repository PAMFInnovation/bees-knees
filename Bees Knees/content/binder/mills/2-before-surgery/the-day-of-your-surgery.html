The Day of Your Surgery

  • Shower in the morning, again using the antiseptic sponges you were given by the pre-admission nurse. Don’t use hairspray, hair gel, deodorant or makeup. Do not shave.
  • Brush your teeth and rinse, but do not swallow any liquid.
  • Take any medication you were instructed to take with just a SIP of water.
  • Do not wear contacts. Bring a case for your glasses.
