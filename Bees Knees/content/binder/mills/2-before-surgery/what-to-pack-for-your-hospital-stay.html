What to Pack for Your Hospital Stay

Wear comfortable clothing and loose-fitting pants to the hospital. You can wear the same outfit on your return home. A shirt or top that buttons or zips in front is advisable. At the hospital you will receive a gown, pajama bottoms and a pair of non-skid slipper socks. Please pack lightly as storage is minimal. The hospital provides basic toiletries, but you may bring your own if you prefer.

Toiletries

  • Dental care
  • Shaving items
  • Lip balm
  • Hairbrush and/or comb

Clothing

  • Change of underclothing
  • Loose-fitting pants or shorts and shirt that are easy to pull on
  • Sneakers without laces or any slip-on shoes that cover your heel

Assistive Devices

  • Dentures
  • Glasses
  • Hearing aids with extra batteries

Please bring this binder with you to the hospital.
