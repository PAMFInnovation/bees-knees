Knee Exercises

Ankle Pumps

Bend ankles to move feet up and down, alternating feet. 10 times per hour.

For the following exercises work up to 3 sets of 10 twice a day

Quad Sets

Slowly tighten muscles on thigh of operated leg while counting out loud to 10. Relax. Repeat.

Heel Slides

Bend knee and pull heel toward buttocks. Hold for 10 seconds. Relax. Repeat.

Long Arc Quads

Straighten operated leg and try to hold it for 10 seconds. Relax. Repeat.

Knee Slides

Slowly slide your foot back as far as you can and hold 10 seconds. Relax. Repeat.

Short Arc Quads

Place a rolled towel under your knee. Raise the lower part of your leg until your knee is straight. Hold for 10 seconds. Relax. Repeat.

Straight Leg Raises

Bend non-operative leg. Keep other leg as straight as possible and tighten muscles on top of thigh. Slowly lift straight leg 6-8 inches from bed and hold for 10 seconds. Relax. Repeat.
