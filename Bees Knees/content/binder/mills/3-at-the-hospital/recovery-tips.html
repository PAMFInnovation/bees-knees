Recovery Tips

• Work with your nurses and therapists to keep your pain under control, so you can move around as much as possible.

• Spend time out of bed each day. With help from your nurses and therapists, get out of bed 2-3 times each day and walk for short distances or sit in a chair at the bedside.

• Change your position, cough, and take deep breaths every 2 hours in bed.

• Use the compression pumps in bed.

• Do your ankle pump exercises every hour in bed.

• Use the Continuous Passive Motion (CPM) machine if your doctor has ordered it.

Ankle Pumps

Bend ankles to move feet up and down, alternating feet.
