The Day You Go Home

Discharge is typically at 11 a.m. The day of your discharge, the surgeon and the hospitalist doctor will come by for important health checks. Ask your doctor or nurse about:

• Any new medications you will be taking

• Warning signs for when to call your doctor

• Your follow-up care and post-op appointment with your doctor

• What kinds of activities are safe to do

• What you can do to help prevent infection at the surgical site

• NOTE: You may not go home alone in a taxi or on a bus. Arrange for someone to take you home and stay with you for a few days.
